Our Mission

LexiNote is built to make reading more inclusive. Dyslexia affects how the brain processes written language, often making letters appear to move, flip, or blur together. Our platform reduces these barriers through specialized design.

The platform follows the British Dyslexia Association (BDA) guidelines—focusing on high-contrast clarity, increased letter spacing, and left-aligned text to reduce visual stress.

LexiNote utilizes the OpenDyslexic Font. Unlike standard fonts, OpenDyslexic uses weighted bottoms to give letters a "gravity" that helps the eye stay on track and prevents common reversals of letters like 'b' and 'd'.

Whether you are converting notes, identifying handwriting errors, or practicing writing, LexiNote is your partner in accessible education.
